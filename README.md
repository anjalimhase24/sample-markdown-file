# How to Access Course 📚

Welcome to the RoadToCode Course!

Follow these simple steps to access your course:

## Step 1: Visit the Website 🌐

Open the RoadToCode website:
https://roadtocode.org

## Step 2: Login 🔐

Click on **Login** and sign in using the email address or mobile number you used during registration.

## Step 3: Open Dashboard 📂

After logging in, go to your **Dashboard**.

## Step 4: Access Your Course 🚀

You will find all your enrolled courses in the dashboard.
Click on your course to start learning.

## Need Help? 🤝

If you are unable to access your course, contact the RoadToCode support team.

Happy Learning! 💙
